ABOUT us

Preserving the Excellency

Est. 2001

A long time ago...

A group of old friends decided to start a small moving business. We share the same values as our founders- Affordability, Service Excellence and Agility. Our goal is to help Calgarians move into their new homes hassle-free and securely. We are a small, local company and we cherish the Calgary community spirit in our work.

our process

What happens after we get in touch

  • 1

    Estimate

    We calculate an estimate based on the loaction, stairs, amount of rooms with furniture and account for any exceptionally heavy items.

  • 2

    Contract

    Once the agreement is set up, we seal it with signatures. We take a half of the amount as a pre-payment.

  • 3

    Move

    We arrive on the set day/ time and complete the move. The final payment amount is fulfilled.

  • 4

    Review

    We kindly ask you to review your experience and pass our business cards to your connections.
